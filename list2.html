Top 10 Pizza Places in Chicago

1. Lou Malnati's Pizzeria 439 N Wells St, Chicago, IL 60654

  1. Lou Malnati's is an iconic Chicago pizzeria known for its deep-dish pizza with a buttery crust, gooey cheese, and flavorful sauce. Their commitment to using fresh ingredients and a secret family recipe for the dough consistently delivers a mouthwatering pizza experience, earning them the top spot on this list.

2. Giordano's 223 W Jackson Blvd, Chicago, IL 60606

  1. Giordano's is renowned for its stuffed deep-dish pizza, boasting layers of cheese, toppings, and a buttery crust. With a history dating back to 1974, Giordano's has perfected the art of deep-dish pizza, making it a must-visit for pizza aficionados in Chicago.

3. Pequod's Pizza 2207 N Clybourn Ave, Chicago, IL 60614

  1. Pequod's is famous for its caramelized crust, created by baking cheese along the edges of their deep-dish pizzas. This unique touch, coupled with high-quality ingredients and generous toppings, has made Pequod's a beloved institution among locals and visitors alike.
4. Piece Brewery and Pizzeria 1927 W North Ave, Chicago, IL 60622
  1. Offering a blend of New Haven and Chicago-style pizzas, Piece Brewery and Pizzeria combines delicious thin-crust pizza with craft beer brewed on-site. The lively atmosphere, tasty pies, and refreshing brews make it a hotspot for pizza and beer enthusiasts.
5.Art of Pizza 3033 N Ashland Ave, Chicago, IL 60657
  1. Art of Pizza is acclaimed for its crispy thin-crust pizzas loaded with a variety of fresh toppings. Their commitment to quality and consistency has earned them a loyal following, cementing their reputation as one of Chicago's top pizza destinations.
6.Louisa's Pizza & Pasta 14025 S Cicero Ave, Crestwood, IL 60418
  1. Louisa's Pizza & Pasta offers a diverse menu featuring traditional thin-crust pizzas, flavorful pasta dishes, and hearty sandwiches. With friendly service and mouthwatering pizzas made from scratch, it's a hidden gem worth seeking out.
7.Labriola Chicago 535 N Michigan Ave, Chicago, IL 60611
  1. Labriola Chicago specializes in artisanal pizzas crafted from high-quality ingredients and baked to perfection in a wood-fired oven. Their commitment to using fresh, locally sourced ingredients results in pizzas bursting with flavor, making it a favorite among discerning pizza connoisseurs.
8.Vito & Nick's Pizzeria 8433 S Pulaski Rd, Chicago, IL 60652
  1. Vito & Nick's Pizzeria has been serving up delicious thin-crust pizzas since 1946. With a crispy, yet tender crust and a wide range of topping options, their pizzas have earned rave reviews from generations of Chicagoans, solidifying their place as a classic pizza joint in the city.
9.Santullo's Eatery 1943 W North Ave, Chicago, IL 60622
  1. Santullo's Eatery offers a mouthwatering selection of New York-style pizzas, featuring thin, hand-tossed crusts and fresh, flavorful toppings. With a cozy atmosphere and consistently delicious pies, it's a neighborhood favorite that keeps customers coming back for more.
10. Nancy's Pizza 2930 N Broadway, Chicago, IL 60657
  1. Nancy's Pizza is celebrated for its stuffed deep-dish pizzas, loaded with layers of cheese, toppings, and tangy tomato sauce. Established in 1971, Nancy's commitment to quality and innovation has made it a beloved institution in the Chicago pizza scene.
